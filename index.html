FAQ

Questions doctors
always ask us.

Does it work on my existing clinic number?
+

Yes — Slotify AI works on your existing landline or mobile number. There is no need to change your number, buy new hardware, or ask patients to use a different contact. Everything continues exactly as before, except every call gets answered.

What if a patient has an emergency?
+

Slotify AI is trained to identify emergency language and immediately escalates those calls — either transferring to you directly or providing the nearest emergency contact. Routine bookings are handled autonomously; emergencies are always flagged.

Can I customise what Slotify AI says?
+

Absolutely. Slotify AI's greeting, clinic name, available services, working hours and conversation style are all fully customised for your clinic before go-live. She introduces herself using your clinic's name and follows your specific workflow.

What languages does Slotify AI speak?
+

Currently Hindi and English, with automatic switching mid-call based on the patient's preference. Regional language support for Marathi, Tamil, Telugu and Kannada is on our roadmap.

How long does setup take?
+

Setup is completed within one working day. We handle everything — connecting to your number, configuring Slotify AI for your clinic, syncing your Google Calendar and running a test call with you before going live.

Is my patient data secure?
+

Yes. All patient data is encrypted, stored securely and never shared with third parties. Slotify Care is designed with healthcare data sensitivity in mind. You retain full ownership of all your patient information at all times.
